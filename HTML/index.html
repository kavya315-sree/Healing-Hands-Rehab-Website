Physical Therapy

  • Physical therapy focuses on improving movement, strength, and functionality. It often involves exercises, stretches, manual therapy, and the use of equipment to aid recovery. Common conditions treated include: Musculoskeletal injuries (sprains, fractures), Post-surgical recovery (joint replacements), Neurological conditions (stroke, Parkinson's disease) and Chronic conditions (arthritis). It involves a thorough assessment of the patientâ€™s condition, which includes reviewing their medical history, conducting a physical examination to evaluate range of motion, strength, flexibility, and posture, and assessing their ability to perform daily activities.

Speech and Language Virtual Therapy

  • Speech and language virtual therapy is an innovative approach that uses technology to provide speech and language therapy services remotely. This form of therapy is conducted through video conferencing platforms, allowing therapists and clients to interact in real-time from different locations. In a typical session, the therapist uses a computer, tablet, or smartphone to connect with the client. The virtual setting offers a range of interactive tools and activities, such as digital flashcards, games, and worksheets, which can be shared on the screen to make the sessions engaging and effective.

Massage Therapy

  • Massage therapy involves various techniques to manipulate the muscles and other soft tissues of the body. Unlike acupressure, which focuses on specific points, massage therapy often involves broader, more generalized strokes. Types of massage therapy we offer are Swedish Massage, Hot Stone Massage, Thai Massageand Trigger Point Massage. It helps reduce stress, anxiety, muscle tension and pain making it effective for managing conditions such as chronic back pain, arthritis, and fibromyalgia. By improving blood circulation, massage therapy promotes faster healing of injured tissues and reduces inflammation.
